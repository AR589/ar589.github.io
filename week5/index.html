  • Legible

Hallmarks of Good Typography

  • Strong hierarchy
  • Plenty of white space
  • A proportional line length (or measure)

Fonts in the Browser

  • The browser will render fonts that exist on the user’s hard drive.
  • There are certain 'web safe' fonts that come bundled with most operating systems.
  • We can load in 'non-standard' fonts with @font-face or through services like Google Fonts or Typekit.
