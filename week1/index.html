Interactive Design — Week 1

Class Overview

Goals for this class

  • Develop an understanding of the interactive design process.
  • Adapt your design skills to the screen.
  • Use code as your design tool.
  • Put your work on the web.
  • Become a better designer.

Assignment 1 — Art Directed Article

Design Brief

Find a newspaper or magazine article and create a digital experience using HTML and CSS.

Goals

  • Create an engaging experience.
  • Enhance the viewer's understanding of the content.
  • Push your boundaries.
  • Have fun!

Examples

  • 41 Days
  • Driving Uber
  • Where’s the Beef?
  • Daft Punk
  • Snow Fall: The Avalanche at Tunnel Creek
  • The Boat
