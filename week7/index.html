AR 589 — Week 7

Mixing Color with Paint / Ink

  • Subtractive color mixing
  • Start with primary colors
  • The more you add, the closer you get to brown

Mixing Color with Light

  • This is additive color mixing
  • The more you add the close you get to white
  • Screens are made up of tiny lights – pixels – that light up red, green, or blue.
  • Resolution is the number that refers to how many pixels are on a display.

Color in the Browser

  • rgba(0, 0, 0, 1)
  • #000000
  • hsl(0, 0, 0, 1)
  • Named Colors

What's Possible?

  • Gradients
  • Filters
  • Media Queries
