AR 589 — Week 9

Intro to JavaScript

What the heck is JS?

  • Gives us the ability to dynamically interact with the browser and the user.
  • The most commonly used programming language in the world.
  • A scripting language: JS runs programs made up of individual steps.
  • Built into all of the major browsers.

Using JavaScript

  • We author files with an extension of “.js” and include them with our HTML.
  • Inside the JS document, we give the browser instructions about what we want it to do and when.
